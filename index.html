About

Benjamin Kamps, born in New York City in 1999. He is originally from Germany and raised in Rome.

The use of organic materials enhances an emotional resonance with his work as well as establishing a profound connection with the natural environment and his childhood. By integrating elements from the natural world, he contributes to a broader conversation of the significance and beauty of the environment.

His language, identified by a research that reflects on the conceptual architecture of human existence. It is characterized by an incessant quest for new expressive forms and renewed awareness of socio-cultural dynamics.

The process, incorporates an instinctive and direct physicality. These actions are manifested with perseverance, care and diligence serving the means to cultivate mindfulness and growth.
